Short Bio

I am a doctoral student at the Faculty of Statistics at TU Dortmund, under the supervision of Prof. Carsten Jentsch. I did my masters in Economics from the University of Mannheim and my bachelors in Economics and Business from the BRAC Unviversity. I love working with causality and causal models, my current work is related to bootstrap confidence intervals for different treatement effect models.
